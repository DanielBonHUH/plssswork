Senior High School Strands

Rosales National High School is a public institution in Rosales, Pangasinan, Philippines, committed to providing quality education to students. The school offers various academic strands in Senior High School, preparing learners for higher education or future careers through specialized tracks like STEM, ABM, GAS, HUMSS, and TVL.

STEM: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. This strand is for students interested in science, math, and technical careers.

ABM: Accountancy, Business, and Management. This strand is for aspiring entrepreneurs and business professionals.

HUMSS: Humanities and Social Sciences. Ideal for students interested in writing, teaching, or social work.

GAS: General Academic Strand. This is for students who want a flexible path in any field, providing a broad foundation for college or work.

TVL: Technical-Vocational-Livelihood. Perfect for those who want to gain practical skills for immediate employment.

ICT: Information and Communications Technology. This strand focuses on technical skills in computing, software, and telecommunications.

HE: Home Economics. This strand prepares students for careers in hospitality, cooking, and other home-related services.

SMAW: Shielded Metal Arc Welding. This strand teaches welding skills for a career in construction and industrial work.
